Web Zines and the Experimental Web

Handcoding Round Robin Part II

"A little bird told me, “Hyperlinks are the oxygen of the web.” How many links can you make in one writing (poem, prose, manifesto, etc.)?" - Laurel Schwulst

Let's do these in 3 rounds:


    Round 1 - 2min each
  • Collect the initial research you've done for your Zine and present the idea to your buddy


    • Round 2 - 20 min
  • Gather the content you have in a .zip file and pass it on to your buddy. You will receive their content. Do your best to add content to your buddy's theme. Put them in the same folder and organize the files. What does your buddy's zine remind you off?
      You can contribute by:
    • Adding images or references
    • Add links to what you think could be useful
    • Write something, collect your thoughts about what they intend to do


    • Round 3
  • You are going to jumpstart your buddies' zine. Put together a folder structure with an index.html, a styles.css and an images folder. Compress the foder and send it back to them.
