Homework: Polishing your Zine 1

Put together your first page for your zine.

It does not have to be your cover or main page, whatever you have ready at the moments.

Things to strive for:

    • Select a layout structure that aligns with your content
    • Explore using display: flex and display: grid in at least one element on your page
    • Plan your navigation. Is it gonna be a scroll showing all your content or are you going to create a page flip-like experience? Start sketching ideas for how you are going to interact with the page you put together.
    • Explore position: relative and position: absolute playing with top, right, left, and bottom properties
    • Publish your page and upload it to Github
