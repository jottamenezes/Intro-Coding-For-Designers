Click on the link and follow the commented instructions to add your own section on the page for interactivity. We will engage in some collective digital gardening together to attempt to make a large interactive map with images and messages from each other to each other.

Homework: Portals

For our last homework, make a page that consists of 3 portals. When you click on the portal, something appears or transforms. Use the templates from our activity today to help you get started. What kind of world can exist here? What does it look, feel, smell, taste like? Utilize our combined skills throughout the course to incorporate interactivity.
