Guidelines for Sharing

  • Spend about 5 minutes sharing your journey throughout the class
  • This can be anything from the folder that you keep all of the creative prompts in
  • Maybe there was a particular discussion that you took away a meaningful sentiment from that you would like to share
  • Maybe there wasn't enough time in your schedule to create files for homework but you thought of something else thats cool to talk about
  • Any sharing of all resolved forms are welcome

Farewell World Activity

As a class, we are going to create "the last page of the internet".

Submit .zip files here

Your task is to create a page that is a last page of the internet. Leave a space for an a tag that will be a link for the next persons page that will lead to a tunnel of links as a final sentiment at the end of our webzine. It will be like a tunnel of farewells.
